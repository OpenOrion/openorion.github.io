Designed for extendibility, collaboration, and simplicity

At Open Orion, we firmly believe that fostering innovation requires the essential elements of extensibility, free accessibility, and ease of initiation.

Open

All geometry generation, simulation automation, and ML dataset curation and training are openly available.

Extensible

Crafted with maximum versatility in mind, ensuring usability across a wide range of applications.

Ease of Use

Unlike many other open-source simulation and generative cad libraries, getting started in a Python notebook is exceptionally effortless.

Closing the Design Loop

Seamlessly manage every facet of the design process, from initial user specifications to the generation of CAD models and comprehensive BOMs, refined with simulations and machine learning algorithms.

Simulation Driven Design

Harnessing the power of machine learning and simulation techniques to uncover hidden patterns in large-scale design problems, streamlining analysis, gaining valuable insights, and optimizing the design process.
